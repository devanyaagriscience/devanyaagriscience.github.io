# Biotech Innovation Seminar

## Advancing Agriculture through Science
Held in Lucknow, the **Biotech Innovation Seminar** brought together leading scientists, agronomists, and industry experts. The focus was on the role of biotechnology in combating climate change effects on agriculture.

## Research Findings
Our Chief Scientist presented groundbreaking research on:
- **Drought Resistance**: New gene markers identifying drought tolerance in rice.
- **Bio-fortification**: Enhancing the nutritional content of staple crops like wheat and pulses.

## Future Roadmap
Devanya Agri Science pledged to increase R&D spending by 20% to accelerate the development of climate-resilient seeds that can secure food for the future.
