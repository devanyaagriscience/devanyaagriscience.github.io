# Onion Gavran

## Overview
**Onion Gavran** is the classic pungent onion beloved for its intense flavor. It is a hardy variety that thrives in local conditions, producing bulbs with a strong aroma essential for traditional cuisine.

## Key Features
- **High Pungency**: Very strong, spicy flavor profile.
- **Drought Hardy**: Can withstand periods of low water availability.
- **Traditional Taste**: The preferred choice for authentic Indian curries.

## Technical Specifications
| Characteristic | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Maturity** | 100-110 Days |
| **Bulb Weight** | 60-80 grams |
| **Shape** | Flat Round |
| **Season** | Rabi |

## Cultivation Tips
Requires less water than hybrid varieties. Ensure good spacing to allow bulb development. Good potential for local market sales.
