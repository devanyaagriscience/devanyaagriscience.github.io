# Paddy DAS 300

## Overview
**Paddy DAS 300** is a premier research variety developed by Devanya Agri Science. It is engineered to provide superior yields across diverse soil conditions, ensuring that farmers achieve consistent productivity even in fluctuating environments.

## Key Features
- **High Yield Potential**: Genetically optimized for maximum grain production.
- **Stress Tolerant**: Performs well under varying water and soil stress conditions.
- **Uniform Grain Size**: Produces aesthetically pleasing, uniform grains that command better market prices.
- **Disease Resistance**: Moderate resistance to common bacterial and fungal infections.

## Technical Specifications
| Characteristic | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Duration** | 120-125 Days |
| **Plant Height** | 100-110 cm |
| **Grain Type** | Medium Slender |
| **Sowing Season** | Kharif |

## Cultivation Tips
For optimal results, ensure proper field preparation and adhere to the recommended fertilizer schedule. Water management is crucial during the panicle initiation stage to maximize yield.
