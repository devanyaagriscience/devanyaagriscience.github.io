# Paddy DAS 8028

## Overview
**Paddy DAS 8028** is an exceptional variety for farmers seeking premium grain quality. It produces slightly slender grains with a great cooking profile, attracting better market prices while maintaining high field productivity.

## Key Features
- **Premium Grain Quality**: Slender grains that look great and cook well.
- **Market Preference**: High demand in local and wholesale markets.
- **High Tillering**: Produces more effective tillers per hill, increasing yield.

## Technical Specifications
| Characteristic | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Duration** | 130-135 Days |
| **Plant Height** | 105-110 cm |
| **Grain Type** | Medium Slender |
| **Sowing Season** | Kharif |

## Cultivation Tips
Ideal for areas with assured irrigation. Apply balanced NPK doses and ensure protection against stem borer during the vegetative stage.
