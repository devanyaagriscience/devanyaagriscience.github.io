# Urad Shekher-2

## Overview
**Urad Shekher-2** is an improved variety focusing on ease of harvesting and disease tolerance. It offers synchronized pod maturity, reducing labor costs and ensuring cleaner produce.

## Key Features
- **Synchronized Maturity**: Most pods mature at the same time, allowing for single-pass harvesting.
- **Disease Tolerant**: Good tolerance to leaf curl and powdery mildew.
- **Easy Harvest**: Pods do not shatter easily.

## Technical Specifications
| Characteristic | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Maturity** | 70-75 Days |
| **Plant Height** | 40-50 cm |
| **Pod Color** | Brownish Black |
| **Season** | Zaid / Kharif |

## Cultivation Tips
Suitable for mechanical harvesting if planted at high density. Ensure field is weed-free during early growth stages.
